Imagine you are visiting a house, and the owner gives you a list of rooms you are allowed to enter and rooms you should not go into. robots.txt is like that list, but for websites. Websites use robots.txt to control which pages are seen by search engines and which ones are kept private.

What does it mean?

  • User-agent: * This tells the robots which ones the rules apply to.
  • Disallow: This tells the robots to stay away from the /private/ area of the website.
  • Allow: This tells the robots they can visit the /public/ area of the website.
